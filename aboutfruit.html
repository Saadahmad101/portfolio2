About Fruit

Strawberries are iconic summer fruits, celebrated for their vibrant red color, sweet flavor, and juicy texture. They are versatile ingredients in desserts, salads, and snacks, offering a refreshing burst of flavor and a source of vitamins and antioxidants.

Apples are crisp, juicy fruits known for their sweet and tart flavors. They come in a variety of colors, including red, green, and yellow. Versatile and nutritious. They are rich in fiber, vitamins, and antioxidants, making them a healthy choice for any diet.

Mangoes are tropical fruits with smooth, golden-yellow skin and juicy, sweet flesh. They're prized for their rich flavor and versatility in dishes ranging from salads to desserts, packed with vitamins, antioxidants, and dietary fiber for a nutritious treat.


Bananas are curved, yellow fruits with soft, creamy flesh and a mild, sweet flavor. They're versatile for snacking or adding to smoothies and baked goods, rich in potassium, vitamins B6 and C, and dietary fiber for overall health.
